BAG ARCHIVE
         2021-ONGOING
Since a few years ago, an obsession of mine took place about bags and from then on I've been creating my own which reflect my interests in micro organisms, natural formations and a little bit of extraterrestrial influence.
For now, my focus will be designing and crafting distinctive functional objects. Creating my own fabrics/manipulations will be the next step to present more intricate details on wearable surfaces.
